Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are your predictions?
Our current accuracy rate is 73.4% based on tracked predictions over the last 30 days. All predictions and results are publicly available for verification. Accuracy varies by sport and prediction type.
Is FXM Elite Predict free to use?
Yes! All predictions and results are completely free. We believe in transparency and want everyone to have access to quality sports predictions without any cost.
How often are predictions updated?
Predictions are updated daily at 6:00 AM UTC. We analyze the latest community consensus data from SofaScore and provide fresh predictions for upcoming matches.
What sports do you cover?
We currently cover 12+ sports including Tennis, Football, Basketball, Ice Hockey, Baseball, American Football, and more. Check our Predictions page for the full list.
How do you generate predictions?
We use community consensus data from SofaScore, filtering matches where 70%+ of users agree on an outcome. This collective intelligence approach has proven more reliable than individual expert predictions.
Can I use these predictions for betting?
Yes, our predictions are designed to inform betting decisions. However, remember that sports betting involves risk. Never bet more than you can afford to lose, and always do your own research before placing bets.
Is there a mobile app?
Our website is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices. We're currently developing dedicated iOS and Android apps for an even better mobile experience.
How can I contact you for partnerships?
We're open to partnerships and collaborations. Please use the contact form above and select "Partnership" as the subject, or email us directly at hello@fxmelitepredict.com.
