Predictions

How accurate are your predictions?
Our current accuracy rate is 73.4% based on tracked predictions over the last 30 days. All predictions and results are publicly available for verification. Accuracy varies by sport, league, and prediction type. We update our accuracy metrics daily.
How are predictions generated?
We use community consensus data from SofaScore, where millions of users predict match outcomes. Our algorithm filters matches where 70%+ of users agree on an outcome. This collective intelligence approach has proven more reliable than individual expert predictions because it reduces bias and incorporates diverse perspectives.
How often are predictions updated?
Predictions are updated daily at 6:00 AM UTC. We analyze the latest community consensus data from SofaScore and provide fresh predictions for upcoming matches. You can check the Predictions page anytime to see the latest available predictions.
What prediction types do you offer?
We offer multiple prediction types including:
  • Match Winner (1X2): Predict which team/player will win
  • Over/Under: Predict total goals, points, or games
  • Both Teams to Score (BTTS): Predict if both teams will score
  • Point Spread: Predict margin of victory
  • Handicap: Adjusted odds for competitive balance
  • Correct Score: Predict exact final score
What does confidence percentage mean?
Confidence percentage represents the percentage of SofaScore users who agree on a particular prediction. For example, 82% confidence means 82% of users predict that outcome. Higher confidence generally indicates stronger community consensus and potentially more reliable predictions.

What is ROI in your predictions?
ROI (Return on Investment) shows the potential profit percentage if you bet 1 unit on a prediction at the given odds. For example, +12.3% ROI means if you bet 1 unit at odds of 1.85, you could expect a 12.3% return if the prediction wins. This helps you evaluate the value of a bet.
